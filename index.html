About Me

My Story

Hey! I'm Jose, an Information Technology Specialist/Freelance Web Designer -n- Developer, and a Problem Solver. I'm the guy you need! Ok, let me tell you a little story about me I promise it'll be short. I have endured over eight years in the information technology field. Don't ask me how I did it but I did! It all started when I was 15 years old and purchased my first Sony Vaio $1,200 PC running a Pentium 3 on Windows Millennium Edition, and the rest was history!

Designs made with Passion

As you can imagine it was a horrible experience and you're probably questioning, what in the world is this Pentium 3 with Windows Millennium Edition he's talking about? let me explain, as a kid growing up I had this dream to become a video game developer. Fast forward a few years, when I earned my first computer, I was excited! As I was unpacking the box and putting everything together, and then finally press the power button, viola magic! Fast forward again about three days later, I was experiencing non-stop issues that barely made it functional to do anything like shutting down the computer before I lost my mind.

I was frustrated; I imagined that many individuals had had similar bad experiences, so I pledged that day to pursue a career where I'll advocate for the user. Technology is a way of life for many, and the last thing we need is hitting a stumbling roadblock when something goes wrong. Along in my professional and personal journies and collective experiences has provided incredible insight and wisdom to provide a better user experience.

Why Choose Me?

I chose web development because it’s dynamic, evolving, challenging, and opens up an opportunity for creative ways to any solution. Sure, it's fun to figure how to translate brilliant ideas and manifest into reality with code. However, it's deeper than that I want to get to know you listen to your thoughts understand what problems you may have and determine just beyond having a web presence but how technology as a whole can provide you with the answer. The web landscape is full of noise and visibility to attract your audience has to be done strategically with patience and direction.

I know this because I'm experiencing it! Yeah, there are many things to consider than just a website like SEO, digital marketing, and social media it can certainly be an overwhelming undertaking. Don't worry, I'm here to help. I will be there every step of the process by projecting the work I’m involved in into a living beautiful thing that drives your brand success. Oh right, regarding how I wrote earlier that it was going to be short, well I do appreciate you took the time to get a snippet into my personality and would love to hear from you.
