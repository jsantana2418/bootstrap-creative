About Myself

Hi! I'm Jose, an Information Technology Specialist/Freelance Web Developer/Designer/Problem Solver, and an aspiring Front End Web Developer. I'm the guy you need, really! Ok, let me tell you a little story about me I promise it'll be short. I have endured over eight years in the information technology field. Don't ask me how I did it but I did! It all started when I was 15 years old and purchased my first Sony Vaio $1,200 PC running a Pentium 3 on Windows Millennium Edition and the rest was history!

As you can imagine it was a horrible experience and you're probably questioning, "what in the world is this Pentium 3 with Windows Millennium Edition he's talking about?" let me explain, as a kid growing up I had this dream to become a video game developer because like many boys we sat in front of the TV and played competitively against our friends, and at times thrown a fit for losing. Fast forward a few years, when I earned my first computer I was excited! I was exhilarated as I was unpacking the box and putting everything together, and then pressing the power button, viola magic! Fast forward just a little bit like three days later I was experiencing non-stop errors that barely made it functional to do anything like shutting down the computer to regain some sanity.

I was frustrated, I imagined that many individuals had had similar bad experiences, so I pledged that day I'll pursue a career where I'll advocate for the user. Technology is truly a way of life for many of us and the last thing we need is hitting a stumbling roadblock when something goes wrong. Along with my professional and personal journies and collective experiences, I acquired provided great insight and wisdom to provide a better user experience.

I chose web development as a profession because it’s dynamic, evolving, challenging, and opens up an opportunity for creative ways to any solution. Sure, it's fun to figure how to translate brilliant ideas and manifest into reality with code. However, it's deeper than that I want to get to know you listen to your ideas understand what problems you may have and determine just beyond having a web presence but how technology as a whole can provide you with the answer. Ultimately, I will be there every step of the process by projecting the work I’m involved in into a living breathing beautiful thing. Oh right, regarding how I wrote earlier that it was going to be short, well I appreciate you took the time to get a snippet into my personality and would love to hear from you. while you're here, check out my projects below and credentials to emphasize how important learning is to stay relevant.

My vision is to continue to do what I love and help small businesses, startups, entrepreneurs, and individuals who want to connect with others that share an important hobby or a cause. Depending on the client's need whether a building a new or redesigning a website and identifying which platform best align with those needs and the business.
